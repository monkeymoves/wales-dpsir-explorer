# SoNaRR 2025 — Navigation Map

**Source:** Natural Resources Wales, *State of Natural Resources Report (SoNaRR 2025)*, December 2025 (updated 28 January 2026), 807pp, NRW, Cardiff.

**Framework:** SoNaRR explicitly uses the **DPSIR** approach — Drivers, Pressures, State, Impact, Responses — to structure its evidence. The 8 Power BI portal spreadsheets carry the same DPSIR structure for the online dashboard.

**Review status (per SoNaRR p.2):** "All content has been reviewed internally and by subject matter experts. Further independent peer review was arranged, with help from the Environment Platform Wales." → **all SoNaRR evidence is factual + expert-validated by default**. The "SME opinion" tag in our mapping template applies only to interpretations *added by NRW staff beyond what SoNaRR says*.

## Practical implications for the mapping work

1. **Every target row will draw from "Bridges to the Future" (p.552)** for the Opportunities cell — that chapter is the forward-look across all evidence.
2. **The dedicated Biodiversity assessment chapter (p.302–325) + its Case Studies annex (p.326–346)** is the single richest seam for Targets 1–8.
3. **Targets 13, 15, 17, 19, 23** are the rows where SoNaRR coverage is thin and the SME/Welsh-context cell will likely carry the substantive content. Worth flagging to Geri/WG in the cover note that these rows are evidence-gap-dominated *by structural design*, not by oversight.
